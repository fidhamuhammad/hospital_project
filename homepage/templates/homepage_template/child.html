Paediatric Critical care

Our PICU /HDU is a 11 bedded level 3 unit manned by a team of two pediatric intensivists with 24/7 cover by pediatric registrars and well trained team of nurses. We have all the state of the art facilities like invasive ventilator, HFOV, non invasive ventilator/ HFNC and advanced monitoring facilities. We are ably supported by all pediatric specialities including neurology, cardiology, nephrology, hemato oncology and pediatric surgery.
